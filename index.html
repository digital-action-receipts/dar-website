Open receipt primitive + commercial self-verifying layer

DAR is open evidence of digital actions. SV-DAR makes that evidence self-verifying in high-stakes environments.

Digital Action Receipts (DAR) are cryptographically verifiable “proof objects” for API requests, approvals, transactions, and AI agent decisions. DAR stays neutral and portable.

SV-DAR is the premium layer built on top of DAR: stronger independent verification designed for adversarial, cross-organization, and audit-heavy workflows. SV-DAR is positioned as the primary commercial product line.

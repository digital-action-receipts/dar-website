Digital Action Receipts

Verifiable, append-only receipts for digital actions.

A Digital Action Receipt is neutral proof that a digital action occurred — created at the moment of action, and verifiable later — without relying on logs, screenshots, or tightly coupled systems.

What this is

  • A minimal, append-only record of a digital action
  • Designed for systems, agents, and automation
  • Verifiable without privileged system access
  • Infrastructure — not a platform

What this is not

  • A monitoring or analytics product
  • A compliance dashboard
  • A log aggregation system
  • A replacement for existing workflows

Why this exists

As automation and AI systems increase, the question “What actually happened?” becomes harder to answer.

Digital Action Receipts provide a neutral, system-agnostic proof layer that makes actions independently provable — across organizations, systems, and time.
